Before You Read

先建立背景，再进入英文

这篇新闻围绕三方展开：BBC 调查、印度政府监管行动、Meta 平台回应。阅读时重点看“谁做了什么、为什么做、如何回应”。

安全提示：本文涉及儿童网络安全与平台治理议题。页面仅用于英语学习和新闻理解，不展示或引导任何非法内容。

Reading Room

原文精读

切换阅读模式：先纯英文快读，再打开中文概括和句子解析。

蓝色：事实信息 橙色：政府/行动 紫色：平台回应
Paragraph 1 · Lead

The Indian government has directed Meta to immediately disable advertisements and content on Instagram that promote or facilitate child sexual abuse material, a senior official said.

印度政府要求 Meta 立即下架 Instagram 上推广或便利传播非法内容的广告和内容。

句子拆解：has directed Meta to... = 已经指示 Meta 去做某事。facilitate 在这里表示“让获取或传播变得更容易”，不是正面的“促进”。
Paragraph 2 · Cause

It comes after a BBC Eye investigation found that Instagram has been running paid adverts promoting child sexual abuse material in India, some of which linked users to Telegram channels where the material was offered for sale.

这一行动发生在 BBC 调查发现 Instagram 出现相关付费广告之后。

句型：It comes after... 是新闻英语高频表达，意思是“这发生在……之后”。比 This happened after... 更正式。
Paragraph 3 · Requirement

The government has also sought an explanation within a week on how advertisements containing such material were allowed on the platform, the official said.

政府还要求 Meta 在一周内解释这类广告为何能出现在平台上。

结构：seek an explanation within + 时间 + on how... = 要求在……时间内解释……是如何发生的。
Paragraph 4 · Meta response

Meta has said it has a zero-tolerance policy on child sexual abuse material and is continuing to strengthen its detection and defences.

Meta 回应称其有零容忍政策，并继续加强检测与防御。

表达：zero-tolerance policy = 零容忍政策。常用于学校霸凌、职场骚扰、违法内容、腐败等严肃语境。
Paragraph 5 · Telegram data

Telegram said it had removed more than 274,000 groups and channels related to child sexual abuse material in 2026.

Telegram 称其在 2026 年删除了超过 274,000 个相关群组和频道。

阅读提醒：新闻常通过第三方平台数据补充事件规模。这里的数字让问题显得更系统化，而不只是单个平台事故。
Paragraph 6 · Official quote

"The government has issued a stern notice to Meta over child sexual exploitative and abuse material appearing in paid advertisements on Instagram," a senior official in India's Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology told the BBC.

印度电子和信息技术部高级官员称，政府已向 Meta 发出严厉通知。

新闻动词：issue a notice = 发出通知；stern = 严厉的。这个搭配适合政府监管类新闻。
Paragraph 7 · Order details

The official further said the federal ministry had ordered Instagram to disable all ads and content promoting or facilitating access to such material.

该官员进一步表示，联邦部门已命令 Instagram 禁用所有相关广告和内容。

表达升级：order somebody to do something 比 ask 更正式、更强硬，常见于政府、法院、监管机构。
Paragraph 8 · Deadline

"The government has sought a detailed explanation from Meta within seven days," the official added.

政府要求 Meta 在七天内给出详细解释。

可迁移句：The regulator sought a detailed explanation from the company within 30 days.
Paragraph 9 · Spokesperson quote

On being asked about the government's notice, a Meta spokesperson said the company has "a zero-tolerance policy for soliciting or sharing" child sexual abuse material, including in ads.

Meta 发言人表示，公司对招揽或分享相关非法内容采取零容忍政策，包括广告。

新闻结构：On being asked about... = 当被问及……时。这是引出公司回应的常见方式。
Paragraph 10 · Detection challenge

"We use advanced AI technology to proactively detect violating content and individuals, but we are in a constant battle with criminals who hide among our 3.5 billion users and try to evade our detection," the spokesperson said.

Meta 称其使用 AI 主动检测违规内容，但也一直在与试图绕过检测的犯罪分子对抗。

重点句型：be in a constant battle with... = 一直在与……斗争。evade detection = 逃避检测。
Paragraph 11 · Timing

The government notice has been issued days after the BBC Eye Investigation was published. However, neither the government nor Meta confirmed whether the notice was issued specifically in response to the BBC's film.

政府通知是在 BBC 调查发布数日后发出的，但双方没有确认通知是否专门回应 BBC 调查片。

短语：in response to = 作为对……的回应。注意新闻中常区分时间上的“之后”和因果上的“回应”。
Paragraph 12 · Investigation details

The BBC set up a new alias Instagram account in India after noticing the platform was pushing sexually suggestive content even when users had not searched for it. The account followed 10 such profiles and, in less than a week, Instagram began showing the alias account paid adverts featuring adult pornography.

BBC 创建了一个印度地区的 Instagram 别名账号，发现平台会主动推送暗示性内容和成人广告。

阅读提醒：这部分解释 BBC 调查方法：新账号、关注特定账号、观察算法推荐变化。
Paragraph 13 · Further findings

Days later, it was also shown adverts promoting child sexual abuse material, some of which linked users to Telegram channels.

几天后，该账号也看到了推广相关非法内容的广告，其中一些导向 Telegram 频道。

结构：some of which... 是非限制性定语从句，补充说明前面的 adverts。
Paragraph 14 · Meta denial

In its response to the BBC Investigation, Meta had said that "child exploitation is a horrific crime" and that it works aggressively to fight it on its apps. The company, however, called it "categorically inaccurate" to suggest it knowingly and deliberately targeted ads featuring children to users with an inappropriate interest in such material and denied prioritising revenue over safety.

Meta 表示儿童剥削是可怕犯罪，并否认故意向相关兴趣用户投放此类广告或将收入置于安全之上。

新闻冲突点：平台承认问题严重，但否认“有意投放”与“收入优先”。这是公司危机回应中常见的辩护结构。

Expression Lab

重点表达实验室

facilitate /fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/

中文：促进；使……更容易；为……提供便利。

原文：promote or facilitate child sexual abuse material

这里的 facilitate 指“让获取或传播非法内容变得更容易”。这个词本身中性，可以用于好事，也可以用于坏事。

常见搭配：facilitate communication / facilitate access to something / facilitate the spread of something

sought /sɔːt/

中文：寻求；要求；试图获得。原形是 seek。

原文：The government has also sought an explanation...

这里不是普通“寻找”，而是正式语境里的“要求对方给出解释”。

常见搭配：seek an explanation / seek approval / seek legal advice / seek compensation

proactively /ˌprəʊˈæktɪvli/

中文：主动地；预先采取行动地。

原文：proactively detect violating content

不是等用户举报后再处理，而是平台主动用技术识别违规内容。

reactively = 出事后再反应
proactively = 出事前主动预防

zero-tolerance policy

中文：零容忍政策。

常用于 bullying, workplace harassment, illegal content, corruption 等严肃语境。

Many schools have a zero-tolerance policy on bullying.

be allowed on the platform

中文：被允许出现在平台上。

适合科技监管、广告审核、内容治理话题。

Misleading advertisements should not be allowed on major platforms.

in response to

中文：作为对……的回应；针对……。

The government introduced new rules in response to public concern.

1. It comes after...

新闻英语常见表达，意思是“这发生在……之后”。

It comes after + 事件
= 这发生在……之后

迁移例句：The new policy comes after several reports raised concerns about online safety.

2. seek an explanation on how...

适合政府监管、公司危机、数据泄露、平台事故类写作。

seek an explanation within + 时间 + on how...
= 要求在……时间内解释……是如何发生的

迁移例句：The regulator sought an explanation within 30 days on how user data was mishandled.

3. be in a constant battle with...

意思是“一直在与……斗争”。

be in a constant battle with + 对象
evade detection = 逃避检测

迁移例句：Online platforms are in a constant battle with scammers who try to evade detection.

Practice Zone

把输入变成输出

完成下面练习后，这篇文章才真正变成你的表达素材。

1. 词汇填空

The government ______ an explanation from the company.

2. 短语替换

把下面句子改得更正式：

The company said it does not accept illegal content at all.
The company said it has a zero-tolerance policy for illegal content.

3. 句型仿写

使用 It comes after... 翻译：这项新规定是在公众对网络安全表示担忧之后出台的。

The new rule comes after public concerns were raised about online safety.

4. 60–80 词英文总结

提示词：ordered / remove / investigation / explanation / zero-tolerance / detection

0 words

The Indian government has ordered Meta to remove advertisements and content on Instagram that promote or facilitate access to child sexual abuse material. The move came after a BBC Eye investigation found that some paid adverts on Instagram linked users to Telegram channels. The government has also sought an explanation from Meta within a week. Meta said it has a zero-tolerance policy and uses AI to proactively detect harmful content.
IELTS Connection

从新闻到雅思素材

Speaking Part 3

Question: Do you think social media platforms should be responsible for harmful content posted by users?

Useful answer: Yes, at least partly. Platforms design the systems that recommend, approve and distribute content. If harmful content is allowed to spread through paid advertisements, companies should be held accountable.

Writing Task 2

Topic: Some people believe technology companies should be legally responsible for harmful content on their platforms. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Useful expressions: be held accountable / platform safety / evade detection / strengthen moderation systems / proactively address risks
